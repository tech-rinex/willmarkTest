Projects under Robotics

Cellphone-Operated Land Rover

The Rover is controlled by an in-built cell phone that is called and commanded by an external cell phone. The dial tone frequency of the button is the input received by the in-built phone, which is decoded into input message by the decoder and processed by the microcontroller to direct the output so that the land rover moves forward or backward or turns around according to the need.

Remote Operated Spy Robot Circuit

The keys of the remote are used to control the functioning of the spy robot. With the press of any button, the encoder encodes the input of the function command, the transmitter transmits it to the receiver and further the decoder decodes it and the output generated operates the spy robot by rotating its motors. The spy robot also has a in-built camera that provides necessary feeds on it existential environments.

Metal Detector Robot

It is basically a wireless circuit principle that consists of transmitter and receiver, with their functions being transmitting and receiving the commands respectively. This allows two major functions of this device, that is stop if any metal is detected and the other being alert immediately by sounding the ringing buzzer tone.
