Projects under Internet of things

Smart Parking System

IoT connectivity help improve parking by locating empty spaces through its sensors. Advanced IoT systems can reserve these parking spaces and even generate electronic payments for this from the drivers. The occupied and empty spaces are gathered as info and updated in the cloud system, and it alerts the driver accordingly to smartly assist the parking of the vehicle.

Air Pollution Monitoring System

Using the smart air pollution monitoring device over a web server with the internet will trigger an alarm and alert the user when the levels of other gases like carbon dioxide (CO2), Benzene, NH3, NOx and others alter to an insufficient/ dangerous level (showing the measures in PPM) judging the air quality index in that particular surrounding.

Smart IoT Alarm Clock

These smart clocks have proved to be way better than the traditional clocks, as they can track and measure you sleep time and quality of sleep, they alert if the person is sleeping in the wrong position to avoid disturbed sleep routines, can automatically adjust time and alarm tones, can play internet radio stations and podcasts, and more importantly they go off only after ensuring your actually awake.
